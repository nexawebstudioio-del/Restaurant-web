SO. Hyderabad

Where street flavour meets the open sky.

We cook the way you'd find in a Lucknow lane, a Bombay maidaan, a Sichuan wok station — then bring it up four floors, into a candle-lit garden where the city becomes background music.

Every plate carries the spirit of where it came from: bold, generous, unhurried — served beneath strings of lantern light with the skyline as your backdrop.
